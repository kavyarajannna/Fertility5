The FERTILATOR is a Medical Training Simulator that has received multiple awards. It incorporates advanced technologies like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Virtual Reality (VR), and 6-DoF Haptics (Degrees of Freedom). This combination of technologies enhances the training experience, allowing users to interact with a realistic simulation using AI, immerse themselves in a virtual environment through VR, and experience tactile feedback through 6-DoF Haptics.
